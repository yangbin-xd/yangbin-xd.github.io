Bin Yang received the B.S. and the M.S. degrees in Telecommunication Engineering from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications, Nanjing, China, in 2018, and Communications and Information Systems from Xidian University, Xi'an, China, in 2021, respectively. Currently, he is a casual academic and pursuing the Ph.D. degree at the University of New South Wales, Sydney, Australia. His research interests include radio map, wireless communications and machine learning.
